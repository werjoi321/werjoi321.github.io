Welcome to the website of the Macro reading group! The objective of the reading group is to create a place of exchange for all macroeconomics enthusiasts. We intend to read papers that appeal to the average macro practitioner.

Before each semester we kindly ask new participants to suggest papers for the group. Regular attendance is not required but highly encouraged.
